# Delivery Playbooks — GCB

---

## 🚚 PLAYBOOK 1: Product Release Sequence

1. Finalize product (book/course/dataset)
2. Publish to GitHub (canonical layer)
3. Announce via Substack
4. Create YouTube content
5. Send email blast

---

## 🚚 PLAYBOOK 2: Multi-Channel Distribution

Every release must go to:
- YouTube
- Substack
- Email list
- GitHub

---

## 🚚 PLAYBOOK 3: Content Recycling

- Convert:
  - Long-form → Shorts
  - Book → Video series
  - Video → Substack post

---

## 🚚 PLAYBOOK 4: Timing Optimization

- Post daily (YouTube + Substack)
- Email weekly (minimum)
- Release major products monthly

---

## 🔁 Rule

No product exists unless:
- It is published
- It is distributed
- It is linked across platforms
