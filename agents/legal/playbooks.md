- Maintain GitHub provenance logs
- Use DOI systems (Zenodo, Figshare)

- Frame as contribution system
- Maintain clear governance docs:
  - DAO_CHARTER.md
  - PROPOSAL_TEMPLATE.md
  - ATTESTATION_LOG.md

## ⚖️ PLAYBOOK 3: Platform Compliance

- YouTube: adhere to content policies
- Substack: proper disclaimers
- Email: CAN-SPAM / GDPR alignment

## ⚖️ PLAYBOOK 4: Business Structuring
